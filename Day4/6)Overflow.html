CSS Overflow Properties

The CSS overflow property controls what happens to content that is too big to fit into an area.

overflow: visible;

The overflow: visible; property means that the overflowed content is not clipped and will be rendered outside the element's box.

This is a container with overflow: visible;. The content inside this container is too large and will overflow outside the box.
